Cherish.
The moments pass. The memories don't have to.
Cherish is a privacy-first digital memory keeping app combining scrapbooking, journaling, voice capture, vision boards, and family sharing into one beautiful experience. Built for families, creatives, bloggers, writers, and anyone who has ever let a moment slip away undocumented.

The problem
Nobody has solved this yet.
People go on meaningful trips, experience important milestones, and live through profound moments — then forget the details. Physical scrapbooking is too time-consuming. Social media is too public. Journal apps lack design tools. No single product combines all three.
⚡
Capture in 60 seconds
Quick snap with photo, note, location, voice memo, star rating and who was there — before the moment fades.
🎨
Design it beautifully
Full scrapbook canvas with drag-and-drop layouts, stickers, frames, ribbons, and caption boxes.
📖
Tell the whole story
6 journal templates with writing prompts. With or without photos. Handwritten Caveat font for warmth.
🌀
Create freely
Vision board, magazine collage, free canvas, photo collage, creative writing board, goal board.
🎙
Speak it instead
Voice capture with real-time transcription via OpenAI Whisper. Audio + text both saved. For anyone whose hands can't keep up.
📚
Keep it forever
Cloud backup, PDF export, print-to-bind hardcover book ordering, family circle sharing.

Private, family circle, link sharing, or public — user controls every memory individually.

The founder
Why Cherish exists.
Two real stories. Both true. Both the reason this app is being built.
"
Last Spring Break we packed three days in Atlanta — the Zoo, the Aquarium, Fernbank, World of Coca-Cola, a Stranger Things tour, and Vampire Diaries filming locations in Covington. It was one of the best trips our family has ever taken. I was so busy living every moment that I ended up texting my husband and daughter photos with locations and dates just so I could document it later. I still haven't. I built Cherish because I needed it that week — not someday.
Ashtin, mom & founder of Cherish — inspired by one very packed Spring Break
"
During my first pregnancy I was diagnosed with Guillain-Barré syndrome. By the time I delivered, I couldn't write. Not a journal entry. Not a single note. I was living through one of the most profound experiences of my life and I had no way to document any of it. My voice was still there — but there was nothing to speak into. I built the voice transcription feature in Cherish because of those months. So that no one — whether they're in a hospital bed, in recovery, holding a newborn with one hand, or simply can't find the words to write — ever has to let a moment disappear just because their hands won't cooperate. Speak it. Cherish will do the rest.
Ashtin, mom & founder of Cherish — this feature exists because of a story I almost couldn't tell

What happens next
Recommended next steps
In order of priority. The first three are free and can be done this week.
1
Get the landing page live today
Go to netlify.com/drop, drag the HTML file in, get a shareable URL in 5 minutes. Free. Post on social media with your real story.
2
Create a Bubble.io account
bubble.io — free account, start with their tutorials. Good for building Phase 1 without a developer. Canvas will need a plugin.
3
Show this document to your developer friend
Everything they need is in here — all 9 screens, full spec, tech stack, build phases, data model. This is your pitch deck and product brief combined.
4
Register the business + grab the domain
Alabama Secretary of State: register Cherish LLC (~$200). Namecheap: buy getcherish.app or trycherish.com (~$12/year).
5
Apply for grants
IFundWomen.com (women founder grants), Alabama Launchpad (up to $50k), Hello Alice (regular small business grants). All free to apply.
6
File a provisional patent
~$1,500-$3,000 with an IP attorney. Gives you 12 months of "patent pending" status. Do this before wide developer outreach.
